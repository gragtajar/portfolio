Side-by-side character details

Cormorant Garamond · Characteristics

✦ High-contrast serif with classical proportions

✦ Feels literary, sophisticated, almost editorial

✦ Evokes: Criterion Collection, art house cinema, Coppola

✦ Pairs beautifully with Geist — creates elegant contrast

✦ Risk: Could feel too "design agency" if not balanced

Bebas Neue · Characteristics

✦ All-caps, condensed, bold sans-serif

✦ Feels commanding, direct, cinematic in a blockbuster way

✦ Evokes: Movie posters, Star Wars title cards, trailers

✦ Strong visual hierarchy — headings pop immediately

✦ Risk: All-caps can feel loud; needs careful sizing

My recommendation: Cormorant Garamond if you want sophisticated & understated.
Bebas Neue if you want bold & cinematic. Both work — it's a personality choice.
