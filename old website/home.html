Other Work
Lenskart Product Data - Figma Plugin

A Figma plugin I independently designed and developed using Cursor to streamline mockup creation for design team at Lenskart.

Why I Built It?

Lenskart's design team was spending hours manually copy-pasting product data.
I created this tool to automate that, saving time.

What It Does?

It batch-updates selected layers in Figma with real product info: names, IDs & images (as fills).

It also includes a General Data tab to insert Indian names, phone numbers, store/customer addresses, staff IDs for designs.
